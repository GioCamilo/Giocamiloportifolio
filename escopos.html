Optimized Projects and Scopes

About the project

In the Escopo Project, we optimized the process of creating projects and scopes within the Industrial Engineering and Maintenance departments at Mangels. The goal was to make scope and project creation faster, more practical, and standardized, eliminating the reliance on Excel spreadsheets filled with complex and hard-to-maintain formulas. With the new solution, the process becomes more agile, allowing projects and scopes to be created in just a few clicks, while improving data clarity and overall organization. Key features include: Structured data storage in a database Intelligent memory, enabling users to reuse recurring inputs and avoid repetitive work Improved organization and management of created projects, supporting more detailed analysis and historical tracking The project continues to evolve with a focus on operational efficiency, standardization, and ease of use for the engineering teams.
